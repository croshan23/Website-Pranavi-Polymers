About Us

Pranavi Polymers is one of the most dynamic, technology driven company, which offers tailor made packaging solutions – complete range of polypropylene (PP) woven bags, polypropylene (PP) woven sacks, high density polyethylene (HDPE) bags and sacks as well as woven fabric.

While our corporate office is situated in Mumbai – the corporate hub of India, we have a state of the art manufacturing unit located at Daman – considered to be sourcing base of PP woven sacks & bags manufacturing industry, world wide.

Woven Sacks / Bags

We manufacture quality PP woven bags / sacks for use in fertilizer, cement, FMCG and textile industries.
